About the League

The 1910 League is a simple idea: We just want to play some old-school baseball on the weekends. In the spirit of old-school ball, we use wooden bats, no batting gloves, and play anywhere we can. If you want a good idea of what we're going for, watch the first nine minutes of this documentary.

Our general skill level is in the “very amateur” range. We're just about playing hard, fast, and anywhere we can (day games only, of course).
